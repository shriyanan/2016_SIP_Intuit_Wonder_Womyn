ABOUT US


OUR MISSION:

With our website, we hope to empower women to believe that no matter their family income level, location, race, sexuality;
that they can succeed in school and in the workplace. We strive to send the message to all women hat they can accomplish
anything they set their minds to.

WHY WE MADE THIS WEBSITE:

As a group of girls who met at an organization called "Girls Who Code," we were shocked by the lack of women in high level
positions, women pursuing a higher education, and women not feeling that they could even enter the workplace.
We know that women can make a difference, just like anyone.
